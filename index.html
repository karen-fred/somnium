Somnium

October 2021

This is a project about the moon, which has been an obsessive feature in my work for a long time. I wanted to write something here that was deeply poetic and head-spinningly philosophical, but it's really not that complicated. Iâ€™m just enchanted by the moon and the subtle power she holds over the Earth and over me.

The sun shows up, clocks in, clocks out, but the moon is the more god-like, transforming sunlight into magic, making the tides dance, castings spells on humanity, imbuing life with rhythm and music.

The skills showcased in this project include: dSLR photography; image processing and editing in Lightroom and Photoshop; paper and digital wireframing using Adobe XD; HTML5 and CSS (flexbox and grid).
